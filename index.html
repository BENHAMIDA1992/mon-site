À propos

[I am a teacher at ESILV and an associate researcher at the Laboratory of Analysis and Applied Mathematics at the University of Créteil. Prior to that, I served as a Temporary Teaching and Research Assistant at Paris Dauphine University-PSL, where I was a member of CEREMADE (Center for Research in Mathematics and Decision Making). I completed my PhD jointly at the Laboratory of Nonlinear Analysis and Geometry (LR21ES08) and the Laboratory of Analysis and Applied Mathematics (LAMA), under the joint supervision of Prof. Habib Yazidi and Prof. Rejeb Hadiji.]
